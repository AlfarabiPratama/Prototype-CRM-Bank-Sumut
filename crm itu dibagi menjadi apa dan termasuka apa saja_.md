# **PEMBAGIAN CRM: TIPE, MODUL, DAN KOMPONEN**

CRM dapat dibagi berdasarkan **3 perspektif utama**:

1. **Berdasarkan Tipe/Jenis** (berdasarkan fungsi utama)
2. **Berdasarkan Modul** (berdasarkan fitur fungsional)
3. **Berdasarkan Komponen** (berdasarkan elemen teknis)

***

## **1. PEMBAGIAN CRM BERDASARKAN TIPE/JENIS**

### **A. Operational CRM (Fokus: Otomasi Proses Sehari-hari)**

**Definisi:** CRM yang fokus pada otomasi dan efisiensi proses customer-facing (sales, marketing, customer service)[^1][^2][^3]

**Fungsi Utama:**

- ‚úÖ **Sales Force Automation (SFA)**: Tracking leads, pipeline management, opportunity scoring, forecast sales
- ‚úÖ **Marketing Automation**: Email campaigns, lead nurturing, drip campaigns, personalization
- ‚úÖ **Customer Service Automation**: Ticketing system, case management, SLA tracking, knowledge base

**Contoh untuk Bank Sumut:**

- Agent buka Customer 360 ‚Üí lihat profil ‚Üí buat case keluhan ‚Üí assign ‚Üí update ‚Üí close (semua otomatis tracking)
- Campaign email otomatis untuk nasabah yang eligible (berdasarkan segment + consent)
- Case routing otomatis berdasarkan kategori (transaksi gagal ‚Üí backoffice payment, kartu tertelan ‚Üí cabang terdekat)

**Platform Contoh:** Salesforce Sales Cloud, Zoho CRM, Microsoft Dynamics 365, HubSpot CRM[^3][^1]

***

### **B. Analytical CRM (Fokus: Data \& Insight)**

**Definisi:** CRM yang fokus pada analisis data customer untuk mendapatkan insight dan membuat keputusan berbasis data[^2][^1][^3]

**Fungsi Utama:**

- ‚úÖ **Data Warehousing**: Konsolidasi data customer dari berbagai sumber (OLIB, mobile banking, cabang, call center)
- ‚úÖ **Data Mining**: Identify patterns, trends, segmentasi customer behavior
- ‚úÖ **OLAP (Online Analytical Processing)**: Multi-dimensional analysis, drill-down capability
- ‚úÖ **Predictive Analytics**: Forecasting churn, next-best-action, propensity models

**Contoh untuk Bank Sumut:**

- Dashboard: "Top 10 root cause keluhan bulan ini" ‚Üí identifikasi masalah sistemik
- Segmentasi: "Nasabah aktif transaksi mobile banking tapi belum pakai QRIS" ‚Üí target campaign QRIS onboarding
- Churn prediction: "Nasabah dengan penurunan transaksi 50% dalam 3 bulan" ‚Üí proactive retention call

**Platform Contoh:** Salesforce Einstein Analytics, Zoho Analytics, Microsoft Power BI (integrated), SAP CRM Analytics[^1][^3]

***

### **C. Collaborative CRM (Fokus: Kolaborasi Antar-Tim)**

**Definisi:** CRM yang fokus pada komunikasi dan kolaborasi antara department (sales, marketing, service, compliance) dan dengan customer[^2][^3][^1]

**Fungsi Utama:**

- ‚úÖ **Shared Customer Information**: Semua department akses data yang sama (single source of truth)
- ‚úÖ **Interaction Management**: Tracking semua interaksi multi-channel (cabang, call center, WA, email, mobile app)
- ‚úÖ **Channel Management**: Manage communication channels yang konsisten
- ‚úÖ **Document Management**: Shared access ke dokumen customer (KYC, contracts, bukti keluhan)

**Contoh untuk Bank Sumut:**

- Sales team share info kebutuhan nasabah ‚Üí backoffice product team ‚Üí customer service follow-up (semua tahu konteks yang sama)
- Case created by call center ‚Üí di-assign ke cabang terdekat ‚Üí supervisor pantau progress (semua real-time visibility)
- Compliance team audit case handling ‚Üí lihat timeline lengkap (transparent audit trail)

**Platform Contoh:** Microsoft Teams + Dynamics 365, Salesforce Chatter, Slack + CRM integrations[^3][^1]

***

### **D. Strategic CRM (Fokus: Long-term Relationship \& Loyalty)**

**Definisi:** CRM yang fokus pada membangun relationship jangka panjang dan customer loyalty melalui personalisasi dan customer-centric strategy[^4][^3]

**Fungsi Utama:**

- ‚úÖ **Customer Lifecycle Management**: Manage dari awareness ‚Üí consideration ‚Üí purchase ‚Üí retention ‚Üí advocacy
- ‚úÖ **Loyalty Programs**: Track reward points, tier status, benefit management
- ‚úÖ **Relationship Intelligence**: Deep understanding customer needs, preferences, life events
- ‚úÖ **Value-based Segmentation**: Prioritas berdasarkan customer lifetime value (CLV)

**Contoh untuk Bank Sumut:**

- Program "Sahabat Sumut" dengan tier (Silver/Gold/Platinum) berdasarkan relationship depth
- Financial wellness journey: edukasi budgeting ‚Üí goal saving ‚Üí cross-sell produk investasi (natural, bukan maksa)
- Life event triggers: nasabah beli rumah (KPR approved) ‚Üí auto-campaign asuransi rumah + home improvement loan

**Platform Contoh:** Salesforce Marketing Cloud + Service Cloud combo, Oracle CRM, SAP CRM[^3]

## **2. PEMBAGIAN CRM BERDASARKAN MODUL (Fungsional)**

### **A. Sales Module (Modul Penjualan)**

**Fungsi:**

- Lead tracking \& management (prospek ‚Üí qualified ‚Üí opportunity)
- Opportunity pipeline management (stage: cold ‚Üí warm ‚Üí hot ‚Üí closing)
- Sales forecasting \& analytics (predictive revenue, win rate)
- Quote \& proposal generation (automated proposal dari template)
- Commission \& incentive tracking

**Untuk Bank Sumut:**

- RM tracking opportunity: nasabah SME butuh working capital loan ‚Üí pipeline stage: initial contact ‚Üí proposal ‚Üí credit analysis ‚Üí approval ‚Üí disbursement
- Dashboard: "Sales pipeline per RM", "Conversion rate per product", "Average time to close"

***

### **B. Marketing Module (Modul Pemasaran)**

**Fungsi:**

- Campaign management (multi-channel: email, SMS, WA, push notification)
- Segmentation \& targeting (rule-based atau AI-driven)
- Lead generation \& nurturing (drip campaigns, lead scoring)
- Marketing analytics (CTR, conversion rate, ROI per campaign)
- **Consent management** (marketing consent gating - WAJIB untuk Bank Sumut)[^5][^6]

**Untuk Bank Sumut:**

- Segment: "Nasabah gaji > 5 juta + belum punya kartu kredit + marketing consent = true" ‚Üí target campaign CC
- Campaign: "Promo QRIS cashback 10% di merchant lokal" ‚Üí send via WA + email ‚Üí tracking open rate + click rate
- Lead scoring: nasabah yang download brosur KPR + visit branch = high intent ‚Üí prioritas follow-up

***

### **C. Customer Service Module (Modul Layanan Pelanggan)**

**Fungsi:**

- Ticket/Case management (complaint handling end-to-end)
- Knowledge base \& self-service portal (FAQ, chatbot)
- SLA tracking \& monitoring (first response time, resolution time)
- Customer feedback \& survey tools (NPS, CSAT, CES)
- Multi-channel support (email, phone, chat, social media)[^6][^5]

**Untuk Bank Sumut:**

- Case management: keluhan "Transfer gagal tapi saldo terpotong" ‚Üí create case ‚Üí kategori: Transaction ‚Üí SLA 24 jam ‚Üí assign backoffice ‚Üí investigasi ‚Üí tanggapan akhir ‚Üí close
- Knowledge base: "Cara aktivasi mobile banking", "Reset PIN ATM" ‚Üí self-service 24/7
- Survey: post-case closure ‚Üí "Seberapa puas dengan penyelesaian?" (1-5 stars)

***

### **D. Analytics \& Reporting Module**

**Fungsi:**

- Customer data analysis tools (behavior patterns, purchase trends)
- Predictive analytics (churn prediction, next-best-action, CLV)
- Data visualization \& reporting (dashboard, charts, graphs)
- Performance dashboards (sales KPI, service KPI, marketing KPI)
- Custom report builder[^5][^6]

**Untuk Bank Sumut:**

- Dashboard Direktur: "Volume keluhan per kategori", "SLA compliance rate", "NPS score per cabang"
- Dashboard Supervisor: "Backlog case per agent", "Average handling time", "Top root cause"
- Predictive: "Nasabah dengan probability churn > 70%" ‚Üí proactive retention action

***

### **E. Workflow \& BPM Module (Otomasi Proses Bisnis)**

**Fungsi:**

- Visual workflow designer (drag-and-drop process builder)
- Approval workflows (maker-checker, multi-level approval)
- Business process automation (routing, escalation, notification)
- Audit trail \& change tracking (siapa ubah apa, kapan)

**Untuk Bank Sumut:**

- Workflow: Case severity High ‚Üí auto-escalate ke Supervisor dalam 2 jam jika tidak ada update
- Approval: Campaign targeting > 10K nasabah ‚Üí wajib approval Compliance sebelum execute
- Onboarding: Nasabah baru ‚Üí KYC verification ‚Üí account opening ‚Üí welcome email ‚Üí assign RM (semua otomatis)

***

### **F. Mobile CRM Module**

**Fungsi:**

- Mobile app untuk on-the-go access (iOS + Android)
- Offline capabilities (sync saat online kembali)
- GPS integration (check-in cabang, location-based promo)
- Push notifications (urgent case, SLA overdue, approval pending)[^6][^5]

**Untuk Bank Sumut:**

- RM di lapangan: kunjungi nasabah corporate ‚Üí buka profile Customer 360 via mobile ‚Üí update meeting notes ‚Üí foto bukti kunjungan ‚Üí submit (sync otomatis)
- Agent cabang: notification "Case baru assigned ke kamu: Kartu tertelan ATM" ‚Üí immediate action

***

### **G. Integration \& API Module**

**Fungsi:**

- REST/SOAP API untuk integrasi dengan sistem eksternal
- Pre-built connectors (OLIB, core banking, mobile app, QRIS)
- Webhook support (real-time event notification)
- Data sync scheduler (batch import/export)

**Untuk Bank Sumut:**

- Integration: OLIB ‚Üí CRM (customer master data sync setiap 2 jam)
- Integration: Mobile Banking ‚Üí CRM (transaction event real-time)
- Integration: WhatsApp Business API ‚Üí CRM (incoming message auto-create case)

***

### **H. Document Management Module**

**Fungsi:**

- Secure storage \& management (KYC docs, contracts, bukti keluhan)
- Version control (track document changes)
- E-signature support (digital signing)
- Compliance document retention (sesuai regulasi OJK)

**Untuk Bank Sumut:**

- KYC docs: KTP, NPWP, slip gaji ‚Üí upload ke customer profile ‚Üí auto-indexing
- Case attachments: Bukti transfer, screenshot error ‚Üí attach ke case ‚Üí immutable storage
- Compliance: Retention policy 5 tahun untuk dokumen terkait keluhan (sesuai POJK)

***

### **I. Security \& Compliance Module**

**Fungsi:**

- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Field-level security \& masking
- Audit logging (setiap aksi kritikal)
- Encryption at rest \& in transit
- Compliance reporting (OJK, PBI, UU PDP)[^7][^8]

**Untuk Bank Sumut:**

- RBAC: Agent hanya lihat data masked, Supervisor lihat full (dengan reason)
- Audit log: "User X access customer Y profile pada timestamp Z dari IP address W"
- Compliance report: "Jumlah consent withdrawal bulan ini", "Case SLA compliance rate"

## **3. PEMBAGIAN CRM BERDASARKAN KOMPONEN (Teknis)**

### **A. Customer Data Management**

**Komponen:**

- Customer database (central repository)
- Data quality tools (deduplication, validation, standardization)
- Master data management (single source of truth)
- Data governance \& stewardship[^9][^10]

***

### **B. Customer Interaction Management**

**Komponen:**

- Interaction tracking (email, call, chat, visit, transaction)
- Timeline view (chronological customer journey)
- Multi-channel capture (omnichannel integration)
- Context preservation (next agent lihat history lengkap)[^10][^9]

***

### **C. Sales \& Marketing Automation**

**Komponen:**

- Campaign execution engine
- Lead scoring \& routing
- Automated workflows \& triggers
- Personalization engine[^9][^10]

***

### **D. Customer Support \& Service**

**Komponen:**

- Case/ticket management system
- SLA policy engine
- Knowledge base \& FAQ
- Self-service portal[^10][^9]

***

### **E. Analytics \& Business Intelligence**

**Komponen:**

- Data warehouse
- Reporting engine
- Visualization tools
- Predictive models (optional AI/ML)[^9][^10]

***

### **F. Collaboration Tools**

**Komponen:**

- Shared workspaces
- Internal chat/messaging
- @mentions \& notifications
- Activity feeds[^10][^9]

## **KHUSUS UNTUK BANKING CRM: FITUR WAJIB**

Berdasarkan best practices untuk **CRM perbankan** (Banking CRM), ada fitur tambahan yang **wajib** ada:[^8][^11][^12][^7]

### **1. Regulatory Compliance \& Audit**

- ‚úÖ Audit trail untuk semua akses data customer
- ‚úÖ Compliance reporting untuk OJK/PBI
- ‚úÖ Data privacy controls (UU PDP 27/2022)
- ‚úÖ Document retention policies


### **2. KYC/AML Integration**

- ‚úÖ Know Your Customer (KYC) verification workflow
- ‚úÖ Anti-Money Laundering (AML) screening
- ‚úÖ Suspicious activity monitoring \& reporting
- ‚úÖ Customer risk scoring (Low/Medium/High)


### **3. Product Relationship Management**

- ‚úÖ Multi-product view (tabungan, deposito, kredit, kartu, investasi)
- ‚úÖ Cross-sell \& up-sell recommendations
- ‚úÖ Product eligibility checking
- ‚úÖ Household/corporate relationship mapping


### **4. Secure Document Management**

- ‚úÖ Encrypted storage untuk dokumen sensitif
- ‚úÖ E-signature support
- ‚úÖ Version control \& audit trail
- ‚úÖ Retention policy automation


### **5. Branch \& Multi-Channel Support**

- ‚úÖ Branch-level scoping (RM hanya lihat portfolio assigned)
- ‚úÖ Omnichannel integration (cabang, call center, mobile, digital)
- ‚úÖ Queue management (untuk customer service)
- ‚úÖ Appointment scheduling


### **6. Financial Analytics**

- ‚úÖ Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) calculation
- ‚úÖ Profitability analysis per customer
- ‚úÖ Portfolio risk monitoring
- ‚úÖ Churn prediction \& retention analytics

## **REKOMENDASI UNTUK BANK SUMUT**

Berdasarkan kebutuhan Bank Sumut (regional bank dengan kompleksitas menengah), ini kombinasi ideal:

### **Tipe CRM:**

- **Primary**: **Operational CRM** (otomasi case management, campaign, customer service)
- **Secondary**: **Analytical CRM** (dashboard, reporting, root cause analysis)
- **Supporting**: **Collaborative CRM** (shared visibility antar department)


### **Modul Wajib (Phase 1 - PoC):**

1. ‚úÖ Customer 360 (profile + timeline)
2. ‚úÖ Case Management (complaint handling end-to-end)
3. ‚úÖ Marketing Automation (campaign dengan consent gating)
4. ‚úÖ RBAC \& Audit Log (compliance)
5. ‚úÖ Reporting Dashboard (SLA, volume, root cause)

### **Modul Optional (Phase 2+):**

6. ‚≠ï Sales Pipeline Management (untuk RM)
7. ‚≠ï Workflow/BPM Engine (complex approval flows)
8. ‚≠ï Mobile CRM (untuk RM mobile)
9. ‚≠ï Advanced Analytics (predictive churn, CLV)
10. ‚≠ï Document Management (full-fledged DMS)
